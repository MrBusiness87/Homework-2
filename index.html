My name is Borjan, pronounced Bor-yēn.

Hello, thank you for coming to visit my page, hope you enjoy what I have done with the place!
I was born inside of Yugoslavia, the summer of 1987. Already had a brother, so my parents had some experience with raising kids. Eventually, in 1992, the Balkan Civil War broke out and we fled to Germany, now I'm fluent in German. Years later we came to Raleigh, NC, USA, while Bill Clinton was President. I studied and learned my third language, and through the years studied hard enough to earn a Bachelors in General Business Administration from NCCU. In years to come I learned spanish and perfected my tutoring technique, also the thirst for knowledge is staying thirsty, mainly that is because of the movie "The Peak".
